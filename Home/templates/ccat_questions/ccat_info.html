C-CAT Syllabus

C-CAT has three sections (Section A, Section B, Section C) of one-hour duration each. Every section will have 50 objective-type questions of 3 marks each (maximum 150 marks for any one section).
Here’s the syllabus of the different sections of test papers in C-CAT :
TEST PAPER TOPICS DURATION
Section A English, Logical Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude 1 hour
Section B Computer Fundamentals, C Programming, Data Structures, Data Communications & Networks, Object Oriented Programming, Operating Systems 1 hour
Section C Computer Architecture, Digital Electronics, Microprocessors 1 hour
